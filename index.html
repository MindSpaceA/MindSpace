About

A practice shaped by care.

Grounded in science, guided by warmth.

Meet the therapist

Dr. Elena Marsh, PhD

For over a decade, I've walked alongside people through some of life's most delicate transitions — grief, anxiety, identity, love, loss, and renewal.

My approach is integrative: drawing from cognitive-behavioral, psychodynamic, and mindfulness-based frameworks to meet each person where they are. The room we share — whether physical or virtual — is a space of quiet honesty, free from judgment.

I believe therapy is not about fixing. It's about understanding. And from understanding, something gentler and more lasting begins to grow.

Our foundation

Mission & values.

Mission

To offer thoughtful, evidence-based psychological care in a space that feels safe, dignified, and deeply human.

Compassion

Every story is met with warmth, patience, and genuine presence.

Integrity

Growth

We believe change is always possible — at the right pace, with the right support.

Services

Care, tailored to you.

Every service is shaped around your pace, your story, and your goals.

Individual Therapy

A private, unhurried space to explore what matters most. Sessions are 50 minutes and scheduled weekly or bi-weekly, depending on your needs.

  • Self-understanding & clarity
  • Emotional regulation
  • Life transitions

Couples Counseling

Rebuild trust, restore dialogue, and rediscover each other. A neutral, compassionate space to navigate the hard conversations.

  • Communication repair
  • Conflict resolution
  • Rebuilding intimacy

Anxiety Support

Gentle, structured approaches to quiet the nervous system. Learn tools that work — in the room and in real life.

  • Panic & generalized anxiety
  • Social anxiety
  • Mindfulness-based techniques

Depression Support

Compassionate, evidence-based care to help you find your footing again — gently, sustainably, at your pace.

  • Mood & motivation
  • Meaning & purpose
  • Behavioral activation

Stress Management

Practical, sustainable frameworks to restore balance between work, relationships, and rest.

  • Burnout recovery
  • Boundaries & priorities
  • Sleep & rhythm

Online Sessions

Secure, HIPAA-compliant video sessions — with the same depth and warmth as in-person care.

  • End-to-end encrypted
  • Flexible scheduling
